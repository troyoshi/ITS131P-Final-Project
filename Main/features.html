System Features

Role-Based Access

Secure login for Administrators, Staff, and Volunteers, ensuring data privacy and correct access levels.

Beneficiary Management

Register and maintain records of beneficiaries to ensure equitable distribution of relief goods.

Inventory Tracking

Monitor relief goods inventory and availability in real-time to prevent shortages during operations.

Evacuation Centers

Manage and track data regarding active evacuation centers and their current capacity/needs.

Reporting System

Generate comprehensive reports on beneficiaries, inventory levels, and distribution histories.

Data Backup

Integrated backup and data recovery system to secure critical disaster response information.
