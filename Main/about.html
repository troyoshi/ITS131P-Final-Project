Background of the Organization

The Citizens’ Disaster Response Center (CDRC) is a non-government organization in the Philippines that promotes community-based disaster management. It operates nationwide through regional centers and partner community organizations.


Purpose and Objectives

The absence of a functional website and centralized database system makes information management less efficient, leading to delays in tracking disaster relief distributions. This system aims to establish a centralized web-based platform with full MySQL database connectivity to improve inventory management, beneficiary tracking, and report generation.
