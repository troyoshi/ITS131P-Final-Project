System Features

Everything the CDRC Relief Tracker offers — built for efficient, transparent disaster response operations.

Core Module 1

Beneficiary Registration & Management

The system provides a structured way to register and manage all disaster-affected families. Staff can log detailed beneficiary information and access it instantly during distribution operations.

  • Register households with complete demographic information
  • Search and filter beneficiaries by location, name, or status
  • Link beneficiaries to specific evacuation centers
  • Track distribution history per family
  • Flag special needs (medical, elderly, infants)

📦 Inventory Dashboard
Core Module 2

Relief Inventory Tracking

Maintain accurate counts of all relief goods — from rice and canned goods to medicines and hygiene kits — across all distribution centers with automatic transaction logging.

  • Add incoming goods with donor and date information
  • Log outgoing distributions and deduct from stock automatically
  • View current stock levels by item category
  • Transaction history for full audit trail
  • Low stock indicators for proactive restocking
Core Module 3

Evacuation Center Management

Track all active and inactive evacuation centers in your area. Monitor capacity, occupancy, and status of each center to coordinate relief efforts more effectively.

  • Register evacuation centers with location and capacity
  • Monitor current occupancy against maximum capacity
  • Set center status (Active, Full, Closed)
  • Assign beneficiaries to specific centers
  • View center-specific distribution summaries

📊 Reports & Analytics
Core Module 4

Reports & Distribution Records

Generate comprehensive reports for administrators and donors. Every distribution is logged with timestamps, quantities, and responsible staff for complete operational accountability.

  • Distribution summary reports by date range
  • Beneficiary status and coverage reports
  • Inventory utilization reports
  • Per-center breakdown of all operations
  • Printable formats for stakeholder sharing
Core Module 5

User Management & Role-Based Access

The system supports three distinct user roles — each with appropriate access and capabilities to match their responsibilities in disaster response operations.

  • Administrators: Full access — users, reports, all records, system settings
  • Staff: Manage beneficiaries, inventory, distributions, and generate reports
  • Volunteers: Assist in registration, update distribution records, view centers
  • Secure login with role-based dashboard views
  • Activity logging for accountability

System Scope

What's Included & What's Not

✅ In Scope

  • Centralized web-based platform for relief operations
  • Role-based access (Admin, Staff, Volunteer)
  • Beneficiary registration and records management
  • Relief goods inventory tracking and availability
  • Distribution activity recording and monitoring
  • Evacuation center information management
  • Reports on beneficiaries, inventory, and distributions
  • MySQL database storage
  • Data backup and recovery functions

🚫 Out of Scope

  • Online donation or payment processing
  • Real-time GPS tracking of relief vehicles
  • Offline desktop application
  • Disaster prediction or weather forecasting
  • Early warning systems
  • Integration with government DRRM systems
  • Automated SMS or Email notifications
