Who We Are

Citizens' Disaster Response Center

CDRC is a non-government organization in the Philippines that promotes community-based disaster management. It operates nationwide through regional centers and partner community organizations, registered as a non-profit, non-stock social development agency.

The organization works to empower communities before, during, and after disaster events — ensuring that affected families receive timely and dignified relief assistance coordinated across multiple provinces.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To promote community-based disaster risk reduction and management by building the capacity of communities to prepare for, respond to, and recover from disaster events — ensuring that relief assistance reaches the most vulnerable in a timely, organized, and dignified manner.

🌟 Vision

A Philippines where every community is resilient, empowered, and equipped to manage disaster risks effectively — supported by transparent, data-driven relief systems that leave no affected family unserved.

The Project

Why We Built This System

The problem we set out to solve, and how this platform addresses it.

The Problem

CDRC previously had a website, but it became inactive and no longer functional. The organization fell back to relying primarily on Facebook and manual coordination for communication and disaster response operations.

Without a centralized website and database system, monitoring distribution, records, and inventory becomes time-consuming and difficult to manage — leading to delays, potential errors, and limited ability to reach a wider audience during critical operations.

The Solution

This project proposes a web-based Disaster Relief Distribution Tracking System that manages beneficiary registration, relief inventory, evacuation centers, and distribution records using a centralized MySQL database.

  • ✓ Centralized web-based platform accessible by all authorized staff
  • ✓ Role-based access for Administrators, Staff, and Volunteers
  • ✓ Real-time inventory and distribution monitoring
  • ✓ Comprehensive reporting for accountability and planning
  • ✓ Data backup and recovery system

PHASE 4

Integration of front-end with MySQL database, role-based authentication, and all CRUD operations for relief management.
