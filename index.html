Centralized Disaster Relief Distribution

Empowering the Citizens' Disaster Response Center to manage beneficiary registration, relief inventory, and distribution records efficiently.

Explore Features

The Challenge

Currently, reliance on manual coordination and social media limits the efficiency of disaster response. Tracking distribution and inventory is time-consuming.

Our Solution

A web-based database system providing role-based access for administrators, staff, and volunteers to seamlessly monitor and record relief operations.
