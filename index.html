How It Works

From Registration to Distribution

A streamlined workflow that keeps every relief operation organized and accountable.

👤
Step 1

Register Beneficiaries

Staff and volunteers log affected families into the system with all necessary details.

📦
Step 2

Manage Inventory

Track incoming and outgoing relief goods in real time with stock-level alerts.

🏕️
Step 3

Assign to Centers

Link beneficiaries to evacuation centers and coordinate distribution schedules.

📊
Step 4

Generate Reports

Admins review comprehensive reports on all operations for accountability and planning.

Core Features

Everything CDRC Needs

Built specifically for community-based disaster response operations in the Philippines.

👥

Beneficiary Management

Register and maintain detailed records of disaster-affected families including household size, location, and special needs.

📦

Relief Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels of all relief goods, log incoming donations, and receive alerts when supplies run low.

🏕️

Evacuation Center Info

Manage information about active evacuation centers including capacity, location, and current occupancy.

📋

Distribution Records

Log every relief distribution event with timestamps, quantities, and recipient confirmation for full accountability.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Generate detailed reports on beneficiaries, inventory usage, and distribution summaries for stakeholders.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Separate dashboards and permissions for Administrators, Staff, and Volunteers to maintain data security.
